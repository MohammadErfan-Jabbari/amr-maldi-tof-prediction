# Experiments — Archive

Historical experiment scripts retained for **provenance and reproducibility of the
exploration**, not for reproducing the best score. These are the phase-by-phase runs,
hyperparameter sweeps, and one-off variants that led to the final approach.

- They have been made path-portable but are **not maintained**.
- Their results are **superseded** by `../run_mega_blend.py` (the best submission).
- The *reasoning* behind what was kept vs. abandoned — and the negative results that
  matter most — is written up in [`../../docs/04_results_and_lessons.md`](../../docs/04_results_and_lessons.md).

Read these to trace how the project actually evolved (baseline → phase sweeps →
rounds → ensembling → semi-supervised/transductive attempts), not as a clean tutorial.
