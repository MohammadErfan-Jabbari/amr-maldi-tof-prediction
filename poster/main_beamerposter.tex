\begin{block}{Problem \& Dataset}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Goal: predict resistance (0/1) for \textbf{8 antibiotics} from \textbf{6,000 MALDI-TOF} features (multi-label).
  \item Metric: \textbf{mean AUC across antibiotics}.
  \item Challenge: \textbf{species distribution shift} + \textbf{systematic missing labels} (semi-supervised setting).
\end{itemize}
\end{block}

\begin{block}{EDA: The 3 Constraints That Shaped Everything}
\vspace{0.15cm}
\maybeincludegraphics[width=\linewidth]{figs/species_distribution.png}
\vspace{0.25cm}
\maybeincludegraphics[width=\linewidth]{figs/missing_label_heatmap.png}
\vspace{0.25cm}
\maybeincludegraphics[width=\linewidth]{figs/sparsity_by_feature.png}
\vspace{0.15cm}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Shift}: train $\neq$ test species mix $\Rightarrow$ validation must mimic test distribution.
  \item \textbf{Missingness}: labels missing non-randomly (esp. Amox/Clav) $\Rightarrow$ always mask NaNs.
  \item \textbf{Sparsity}: MALDI features are mostly zeros $\Rightarrow$ tree models are strong baselines.
\end{itemize}
\end{block}

\begin{block}{Methods (What We Tried)}
\small
\setlength{\tabcolsep}{6pt}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.25}
\begin{tabular}{@{}p{0.34\linewidth}p{0.60\linewidth}@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Family} & \textbf{Takeaway} \\
\midrule
Baseline (LGB) & Solid starting point for sparse MALDI features. \\
Intrinsic rules & High-confidence shortcuts for known species/antibiotic pairs. \\
Species reweighting & Counter shift by up/down-weighting species. \\
PLS / DR & PLS helped; PCA-like DR hurt in our runs. \\
Ensembling & \textbf{Rank averaging} robust; stacking overfit under shift. \\
Semi-supervised & Self-training helped locally; did not beat best rank-avg ensemble. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\normalsize
\end{block}

\begin{block}{Validation Strategy (Avoiding the Trap)}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Held-out validation split matching \textbf{test species distribution}.
  \item Model selection on \textbf{mean AUC across all 8 tasks} (not a single species).
\end{itemize}
\end{block}

\begin{block}{Results \& Lessons}
\small
\begin{tabular}{@{}p{0.62\linewidth}rr@{}}

\vspace{0.25cm}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Stacking overfit}: great OOF, worse leaderboard.
  \item \textbf{Domain knowledge mattered}: intrinsic resistance yields free-confidence predictions.
  \item Private leaderboard improved vs public (rank change: +4 in final screenshot).
\end{itemize}
